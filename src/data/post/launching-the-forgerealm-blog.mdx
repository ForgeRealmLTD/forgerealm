We've been meaning to do this for a while — a proper place to share what's going on behind the scenes at ForgeRealm.

## Why a blog?

Social media is great for quick updates, but some things deserve more than a caption. Booth recaps, the story behind a new design, the messy process of dialling in a print profile — that kind of stuff needs room to breathe.

This is that room.

## What you'll find here

- **Product drops** — first look at new prints before they hit the shop
- **Booth recaps** — photos, stories, and lessons from our market stalls
- **Studio notes** — the real process, including the fails
- **Subscriber updates** — mirrored here so you never miss one

## What's next

We're working on connecting this to our mailing list so subscribers get new posts straight to their inbox. If you're not subscribed yet, now's a good time — we only send the good stuff.

See you at the next booth.

— *The ForgeRealm Team, Leeds*
