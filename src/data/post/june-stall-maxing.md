This is the first month where being a two man business actually feels like a constraint we are actively working around. June is shaping up to be the busiest run we have done so far, four stall bookings and a permanent presence at the Merrion, and the only way we are pulling it off is by splitting up and running two locations on the same day. Stall maxing at its finest.

## Two Saturdays, four stalls

We have booked the same setup twice in June. On Saturday the 6th, one of us will be at Kirkgate Market while the other runs Albion Place. We are then doing it again on Saturday the 20th, same pair of locations, same split. Four bookings on two days. The only reason this is even possible is that we now have enough printers to feed two displays at once without one of them going light by lunchtime.

The 6th is our next stall and we are printing roughly double the stock we usually take to a single market. Two full displays means two full inventories, and we would rather over-prepare and bring stock home than show up to either booth looking thin.

## A permanent shelf at the Merrion

Separately, from Friday the 13th, ForgeRealm pieces will be available at the Merrion mini market. This is a different thing entirely. Rather than a one day booking, it is an always on retail spot where people can drop in throughout the week and pick something up without waiting for the next Saturday. It is the first time our products will have a home outside of a stall, and we are treating it as a small but real step toward proper retail presence in Leeds.

## Why now

We talked in the last post about the Creator 5 changing what we can offer in terms of volume. June is the first month where that capacity actually gets tested in the wild. Two stalls on the same day was not realistic a few months ago when one printer going down meant rebalancing everything. Now, with a fuller farm running in the background, we can take the risk.

We will write up how each of these goes once they actually happen. For now, if you are in Leeds, you have more chances than ever to find us at a market in June, and a permanent option in between.
